SigmaTizm
A Virtual Additive Synthesizer
Version 1.1

The audio settings dialog lets you change the audio output device used by SigmaTizm. The list of devices is generated based on the sound devices available on your system. Selecting a device from the list will switch to it immediately.

Every sound device has a different latency, which is the amount of time between hitting a key and hearing sound. Some devices may have more than one driver with different latency values (for instance, ASIO drivers, WDM drivers, and MME drivers for the same device have different latency). You should choose the driver with the lowest latency if available, otherwise there could be a delay when you play a note, and this delay makes it much harder to play an instrument.

Some drivers also require exclusive access to the audio hardware, so you will not be able to use other audio apps when that driver is selected, or the selected driver may fail to activate when another audio app is running (this includes playing video or audio content in your web browser). This tends to be true of ASIO drivers, which are very fast, but also very greedy.
